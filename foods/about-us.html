About Us

Bountiful Foods

Bountiful Foods is small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, however, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. We sell fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also using some national and international suppliers.
