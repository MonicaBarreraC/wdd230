Site Purpose

The purpose of the chamber of commerce is to promote the interests of small businesses in this locality, as well as to provide diverse growth opportunities for all. Some of the ways in which it will be carried out is through events, organizing meetings between different entrepreneurs to increase their contact networks, improve our locality, etc.
