Target Audience Description

Our target audience is all those small entrepreneurs or those with a relatively new or small business, including those with a limited network of contacts.

Personas

Angela Flores

Angela is a 36-year-old adult woman with children and she opened her bakery about two years ago. Since her business is relatively new her repertoire of connections is small, but it has not prevented her from having a regular amount of clients. She would like to publicize her business to increase the flow of clients and make her business better known.

David Gomez

David is a 22-year-old single student who wants to start a business. He has some ideas about what he wants to do but wants to learn a bit about entrepreneurship so his business can be successful. He doesn't have a network of contacts and would like to start growing it.


Scenarios

  • Where can Angela advertise her business or promote her products?
  • How can David find out about the businesses associated with the Conkal Chamber of Commerce?
  • How often are the companies associated with the Conkal Chamber of Commerce updated?
  • When will the next event be so David can grow his contact network?
  • What does Angela require to join the Conkal Chamber of Commerce?
